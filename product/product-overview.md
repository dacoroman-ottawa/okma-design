# KanataMusicAcademy

## Description
A comprehensive management system for music schools that helps administrators coordinate teachers, students, scheduling, inventory, and payments in one unified platform.

## Problems & Solutions

### Problem 1: Scheduling Complexity
Visual availability tracking for teachers and students with conflict-free class enrollment for both private lessons and group classes.

### Problem 2: Inventory Chaos
Centralized tracking of all products (instruments, accessories, books, apparel) with rental and sales management.

### Problem 3: Payment Tracking Headaches
Credit-based system where students purchase credits upfront and balances are automatically deducted as lessons are taken.

### Problem 4: Scattered Information
Single source of truth for student enrollments, teacher assignments, and account balances.

## Key Features
- Teacher and student profiles with availability calendars
- Class enrollment management (private and group)
- Product inventory with rental and sales tracking
- Credit purchase and balance management
- Dashboard overview for administrators
