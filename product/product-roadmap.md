# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. People
Teacher and student profiles with availability calendars and contact information.

### 2. Classes
Class scheduling and enrollment management for both private lessons and group classes.

### 3. Payments
Credit purchases, balance tracking, and lesson payment deductions.

### 4. Inventory
Product catalog with rental and sales tracking for instruments, accessories, and merchandise.

### 5. Dashboard
At-a-glance overview of schedules, balances, and key metrics for administrators.

### 6. User Administration
Manage application users with roles, active status, and password reset capabilities.
